‚òÖ The National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG) is committed towards maintaining accountability and transparency, and making the Right to Information Act, 2005 effectively operational. We welcome queries under the Act and will try to provide answers through the concerned authorities. This section provides all the relevant details that an applicant will require regarding the Act.

‚òÖ RTI applicants may submit their RTI applications and appeals to the following officers of NMCG:

  • 1. Shri Kundan Lal, Under Secretary & Central Public Information Officer.
  • 2. Shri Binod Kumar, Director and First Appellate Authority.
  • 3. The applications should be addressed to Under Secretary (Coordination), Ministry of Jal Shakti (Department of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation).
  • 4. As per RTI (Regulation of fee and cost) Rules 2005, the application fee can be deposited either through Postal Order/ Demand Draft/ Banker Cheque issued in favour of Pay & Accounts Officer, D/o WR, RD & GR or by Cash.
